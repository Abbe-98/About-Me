Defensive visualization of brute-force protection

Brute force and Hydra in context

See how a login system should react to automated attempts.

This demo focuses on defense. Hydra is mentioned here as an example of a tool that can automate many login attempts in a test environment. The goal is to show how risk should be detected early and how rate limiting, account lockout, MFA challenges, and logging can be activated step by step.
